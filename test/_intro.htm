Most people online consume small amounts of content on the fly from many, many sources. Unfortunately, content providers have never had a viable mechanism for giving consumers access to paid content in a way that matches this wide-ranging, rapid-fire, a-la-carte, cross-domain browsing behavior ... until now.

Glyder enables consumers to access and pay for paid content on the fly across domains as quickly and easily as accessing free content.

To see a demo of how Glyder works:

(1) Visit a "Website"
Click on a demo media site link at the left side of this screen.
 
(2) Choose Something to Buy
Click on any item you wish to "purchase." (Note: Real money is not used.)
 
(3) Log In
If asked to log in, you can use Username: VISITOR, and Password: FASTR. (You only need to log in once.)
 
(4) Instant Purchase and Access
Select the payment option that you want ... and you are given instant access! (This is Glyder's Instant-Purchase-and-Access Capability or "IPAC.")
 
(5) Cross Domain Capability
Now try several demo media site links (at the left) to see how easy it can be to instantly purchase and access content across domains ... without needing to log in at each site you visit.
 
It's that easy!
