About This Demo
 
The vast majority of people browsing the internet consume small portions of a-la-carte content on the fly from many online sources. This is by far the preferred mode of consumption for most people.
 
Unfortunately, media sites have never been able to offer a payment-and-access mechanism that aligns with this wide-ranging, a-la-carte consumer behavior...until Glyder.
 
To see a demo of how Glyder works:
 
       (1) Click on any "media site" link at the left side of this screen.
 
(2) Next, click on any item you wish to "purchase." (Note: Real money is not used in this demo.)
 
(3) If asked to Log In, you can log in with Username: Visitor, and Password: FASTR.
 
(4) To purchase and access any item, simply click on the link for the item, and then select the payment option that you want.
 
It's that easy! Enjoy!
 
Note that the consumer does NOT need an individual account at each site the consumer visits. By enabling a single Glyder account, the consumer gets instant-payment-and-access capability at potentially an unlimited number of websites.
 
And media sites that utilize Glyder are finally able to capture revenue in the deep end of the consumer activity pool: wide-ranging, a-la-carte consumption.
